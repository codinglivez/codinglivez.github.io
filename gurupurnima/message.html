गुरुपौर्णिमेच्या हार्दिक शुभेच्छा

गुरुर्ब्रह्मा ग्रुरुर्विष्णुः गुरुर्देवो महेश्वरः ।
गुरुः साक्षात् परं ब्रह्म तस्मै श्री गुरवे नमः ॥


ज्यांनी मला घडवलं या जगात लढायला,जगायला शिकवलं, अशा प्रत्येकाचा मी ऋणी आहे!

I am an achiever today because I followed in your footsteps. Your faith in me has helped me broaden my vision and discover my hidden strengths.Thank you so much for enlightening me and eliminating darkness from my life.

गुरौ न प्राप्यते यत्तन्नान्यत्रापि हि लभ्यते। गुरुप्रसादात सर्वं तु प्राप्नोत्येव न संशयः॥

I couldn't have gained knowledge from anywhere else. The lessons that you imparted went on to build a strong foundation for my life. I thank you for guiding and showing me the right path


- from Ravivanshikumar Sangpal
