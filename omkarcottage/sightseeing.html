What To See

Alibag - There is Romance in the Air Here!!

Varsoli is just 2.2 Kms from the Alibaug bus depot and 2.8 Kms from Alibag main Beach making Varsoli an ideal place to stay to explore this region of Konkan. There are many beautiful temples historic forts and lot many interesting spots to explore during a holiday in Alibag.

Alibag is a really long stretch of beach and if you are staying close to the beach a long walk on the beach early in the morning is warranted. The north end of Alibag beach extends seamlessly onto Varsoli beach known for its rich avian population.

Varsoli and Alibag is a beach destination ideal to just relax and enjoy a quiet weekend however Varsoli is slowly establishing itself as a destination for water sports enthusiasts. You will find many water rides in Varsoli along with boating and parasailing on the beach.

Apart from the beaches and water sports, the forts and temples near Alibag are the major tourist attraction. The Kulaba fort just off the Alibaug coast, probably is the most visited fort near Alibag. Kulaba fort can be reached on foot via a small strip of land which surfaces during low tides. Murud Janjira about an hour away from Alibag is also worth a visit if you are on a long holiday in Alibag.

Alibag Highlights

Alibag Beach

Alibag has a vast expanse of flat beach with a soft fine mixture of white and black sand. Varsoli Beach, Kihim Beach, Nagaon Beach, Kashid Beach are the famous beaches near Alibag.

Watersports at Alibag, Varsoli Beach

Alibag and Varsoli Beach beach is a popular destination for water sports enthusiasts. All the exciting water rides like banana ride bumper ride and jet skiis are available at Alibag Varsoli beach.

Forts Near Alibag

Alibag is flaked with many forts. Kulaba fort and Khanderi fort are the nearest forts from Alibga beach. Revdanda fort and Korlai fort are near 25 kms from Alibag. Murud Janjira fort is also the most visited fort near Alibag.

Temples Near Alibag

Kankeshwar Mandir is the most visited temple near Alibag. Birla Mandir at Salav is also the attraction for many tourists. Apart from that Jai Bhawani temple, Datta temple, Rameshwar temple are the oldest temples in Alibag.
