How to Reach

Samudra Sneha Cottage is located about 1.5 kms from Alibaug ST stand, along the beautiful sea coast line. Hired Tourist vehicles, Buses, Ferry to Alibaug are easily available from Mumbai and Pune.

By Private Car

The moment you reach Alibag by Pen Alibag road, just before you enter the main Alibaug city you will find the bypass road which is a right from the Reliance Petrol Pump in way to Alibaug. Omkar Cottage is located in Vidya Nagar Varsoli, so if you need to ask for directions, ask for: - Vidya Nagar- Varsoli

By Bus

Take an ST bus to Alibaug. From Alibaug, you can hire auto rickshaws or minidoors (1.5 kms, 7 mins, Rs 50/ Rs 15) to Omkar Cottage, Vidya Nagar- Varsoli.

By Boat (Only From Mumbai)

You can reach Alibaug from the Gateway of India in 1:30 hours - 1 hour for the Gateway of India - Mandwa ferry and 30 mins for the Mandwa-Alibaug bus, (The combined 1-way fare is approx. Rs 100). From Alibaug, you can hire 6 or 3 seater rickshaws (1.5 kms, 7 mins, Rs 50/ Rs 15) to Omkar Cottage, Vidya Nagar- Varsoli.

Nagaon from Mumbai

If you are travelling from Mumbai you have the option of travelling in a boat. There is frequent ferry service from the Gate way of India which would drop you to the Alibaug bus depot. Varsoli is just 1.5 Kms from the depot.

Alibaug town is about 100 kilometres from Mumbai. Once you reach the Alibaug circle take the Alibaug - Revas road. 1.5 kms ahead you will reach a "Royal Motor Training School" in Vidya Nagar. In front of Royal Motor Training School you see Madhugandha Resort Turn left onto Vidya Nagar Road within 2mins you reach Old Muka Badhir School, take first right from it and the first gate is of Omkar Cottage's.
