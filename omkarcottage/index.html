Welcome to Omkar Cottage

Located in one of the most colorful locales in Alibag, Omkar Cottage - Varsoli is an ideal stay option for those looking for a comfortable accommodation near Alibag beach.

Omkar Cottage is a small cottage with spacious self contained AC and non AC rooms nestled within a campus of lush evergreen trees. The cottage is located at 1.5 kms from Alibag ST Stand. Built with care the cottage provides all the necessary amenities to ensure a delightful stay experience during your vacation in Alibag. Delicious home made food prepared in the traditional kokani culinary style is served at Omkar Cottage.

Holidays at Omkar Cottage

Varsoli is the ideal place to stay in the Alibaug region to explore this part of Konkan. At just under 2.6 kms from Alibaug Main Beach Varsoli is almost centrally located to visit all the tourist attractions of the region be it the Kulaba sea fort or the Khanderi Underi fort. Varsoli Beach is also 1.4 kms from Omkar Cottage.
