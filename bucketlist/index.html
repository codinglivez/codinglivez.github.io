Welcome Folks !!!
Got something on your mind? Use Bucket List to jot down everything you have. Whether you want to incerase you productivity, decrease your stress levels or just free up some mental space, Bucket List makes it easier to plan your day and manage your life.
Instructions:
  1. Enter the task to be added in the input feild.
  2. The task will now appear in the list below.
  3. Each list item has two options: Completed and Delete.
  4. If you complete the task, the task will be partially faded.
  5. The option is provided to sort your view into two categories. You can select any view you like to have a focused look at your timeline.
