Welcome to Nagaon...

About Nagaon

Nagaon is a silent village located at a distance of 10 km from Alibag, in the Raigad district of Maharashtra. A quiet and peaceful destination for relaxing, Nagaon Beach is a clean place with yellow sand running over a distance of 3kms. The crystal clear waters of this beach offer amazing opportunities for adventure activities. Due to its calm waters, low-tides and plain surface of the beach, it is considered safe and ideal for the various water activities such as swimming and boating.

Weekly Thursday Market is held to Buy local goods at affordable rate.

How to reach Nagaon

From Mumbai: about 118km by road

By road: local MSRTC buses available

By Ferry: Daily services available from 6:00am to 6:00pm from the Gateway of India to Mandwa and Bhau Cha Dakkha to Rewas ferry.
From Mandwa to Alibaug Ferry operators also provide bus service which is 10km from Nagaon
RORO service is available from ferry bhaucha dhakka to Mandwa

By Train: nearest Railway Stations are Panvel or Pen from Mumbai & Khopoli from Pune

Places near by..

Nageshwar Mandir, Nagaon

This temple was made by Jankoji Aangre as per told by local peoples. It is naturally a beautiful place, in front of Shiva mandir the lake gives the fantastic scene of natural beauty. On the occasion of Tripurari poornima people used to light many lamps on the Deepmal. This Temple is made up of black stone.

Vankhnath Mandir, Nagaon

It is the beautiful temple of Lord Shiva build by Keshav Mahajan by economic support of Ahilyabai Holkar in 1790. There is Samai made of black stone near the temple. There is a beautiful lake in front of this Temple.

Dakshinmukhi Mandir, Nagaon

It is a beautiful temple of goddess Dakshinmukhi. In all temples the faces of gods are facing East or West direction but in this temple Goddess Dakshinamukhi facing at South. Every year People celebrates Navaratri festival with great joy.

Naunag Mandir, Nagaon

Name of village Nagaon is derived from this Nau Nag temple. There are actually 10 statues of black stone in which 9 statues are of Nag Devta and one of Rakshak. Peoples in Nagaon celebrates Nag panchami every year with great joy.

Shitala Devi Mandir, Chaul

9km from Nagaon

The Shitaladevi Mandir is known as the place of Adishakti. This is an ancient temple-Originally it was built with a tiled roof and wooden structure. In 1990, it was renovated into a modern structure. It is also told that the goddess of this temple surrendered to the sea.

Shree Datta Mandir, Chaul

10km from Nagaon

The temple is located at the top of a peak and is reached by climbing around 650 stairs. From atop, the entire region of Chaul and the accompanying Revdanda is visible. The period of Lord Dutta’s birthday is celebrated here with much aplomb over five days.

Rameshwar Mandir, Chaul

8km from Nagaon

According to lore, the Rameshwar Temple was supposed to have been built in a day by the Pandavas, but they were unable to complete it. Centuries later, Kanhoji Angre (Maratha navy admiral, 1698-1729) completed the work.

Revdanda Beach Fort, Revdanda

11km from Nagaon

Revdanda Fort is an ancient Portuguese fort located at Revdanda beach. The fort is located at the mouth of the Kundalika River. Revdanda Fort, also known as Revdanda Aagarkot Killa. Satkhani is the main attraction of this fort.

Birla Mandir, Salav

20km from Nagaon

Birla Mandir is built by the Birla family, in different cities across India. Birla Mandir, Salav is one of them. The temple build with white marble. The garden in temple's premices is nicely decorated with different types of flowers, it is the main attraction for visitors here.

Korlai Fort, Korlai Murud

15km from Nagaon

Korlai Fort is a Portuguese fort situated at Korlai .It is one of the best places of sightseeing in Alibag and also one of the popular historical places. The travellers can visit the Korlai Lighthouse which is still in operation, the area is neatly decorated with garden.

Kashid Beach, Murud

24km from Nagaon

Kashid Beach is situated on the coasts of Arabian Sea. Famous for its beautiful white colour sand and clear water, Kashid is one of the cleanest beaches in north Konkan. Beach have lot of water sports activities

Janjira Fort, Murud

55km from Nagaon

Murud Janjira Fort is located on an island of the coastal village of Murud. The ferrying boats are only way to reach the Fort. However, you need to buy tickets for the boat ride and pay for the parking if there is a vehicle.

Magnetic Observatory, Alibag

11km from Nagaon

Magnetic Observatory was founded in the year 1904. The observatory is one of only 13 such observatories in the world, providing an excellent set of geomagnetic observations. The original observatory was set up in Colaba Mumbai, it was later shifted to Alibag.

Bhimeshwar Mandir, Nagaon

This is Lord Shiva Temple which has a beautiful lake and Deep jyoti stambh in front of the temple. On occasion of “Tripurari Poornima” there is a huge lighting in the premises of temple. There is 28 lines Yadavkalin Shilalekh of 1367 on the steps of this temple.
