Online Examination System

This is an Online system which enables students to register for the examination and perform the tests available on the site whenever they want.

  • Rating:
  • Client: K J College of Engineering and Management Research
  • Project Link: kjeionlinemcq.herokuapp.com
  • Completed: 31 May 2020

Due to the COVD-19 pandemic, the students who have passed the HSC examination will be preparing for the CET tests which occur every year to confirm their entrance for Higher Education. But now the students wont be able to attend the examination due to this crisis and for those students seeking confidence and inspiration this platform is built for you.

Students can register on this platform and complete the exams within the time limits to check their ability at the point. Also eacha dn every student will see a ranking list which will show the sequence of students who have performed exceptionally in this test students can compete for the 1st rank and they will get expert in the field by just practicing these tests. This application is built using Bootstrap framework at the front-end and Python Django at the Back-end.
