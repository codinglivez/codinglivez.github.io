About Us

We organized a State Level SSC [Social Security Convolution] Hackathon from KJCOEMR, KJEI. This event will be completely online and feasible to the students participating in the event. This Hackathon focuses on the students in Diploma colleges all over Maharashtra. SSC Hackathon is held in collaboration with Pune Police as the primary source for inspiration. The problems to be solved by the participants in the Hackathon will be based on the priorities and requirements published by Pune Police itself.

The participants need to submit the idea / solution for the idea of the problem statement predefined or they can freely use their own creativity to widen the horizon of the SSC Hackathon. All the further guidelines as well as the needed information for participants as well as other audience will be published on the website.

NOTE:

There is no need of any working model or a functional unit of the concept; Students should only be prepared with basic documentation material like Synopsis and presentation / research paper.
