Rules and Regulations

Team

  1. 1. Each team can have 2 to 4 members
  2. 2. This event is only for diploma students
  3. 3. All teams will be given equal preference during selection

Event
  1. 1. This competition contains 2 rounds:
    • A. Idea Submission
    • B. Skype Round
  2. 2. Team selection on round two is based on idea which submitted in ppt or pdf with video (if required).
Intellectual

  1. 1. The decision of organizers or Judging panel is final for selection for round two or winners.
  2. 2. You are not allowed to steal idea of another team
  3. 3. All teams will have to submit their ID card's copy with the submissions.
